Anokhi Pehel

Anokhi Pehel is an initiative taken by the students of MNNIT to help the children of the economically weaker sections of the society by providing them an education in the best possible way. Under this initiative, we teach students from preparatory classes to 12th level teaching them various subjects.Not only do we develop academic skills of the students but also other extra curricular activities such as painting, dancing, sketching, etc. Started with a few teachers and just 20 students, today we have a team of about 60 teachers shaping the life of more than 250 students. Our students have shown great performance in various fields.

Stories We Create :


Ishita

This is one of our students, Ishita. Due to poor financial conditions, her family can’t afford to send her to school. Ishita's mother is a single parent and works as a saleslady to make their ends meet. Anokhi Pehel convinced her mother for her admission in Jwala Devi school, prayagraj with full expenditure coverage by college faculties. She is doing great in school and holds a record of getting 1st rank in every session.

Vanshika Jaiswal

“Right mentorship can help you achieve what you always dreamt for.” This story is of a student who got 45% in class 9th and 90% in class 10th (boards). Yes this is something which keeps us going.
